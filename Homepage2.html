Queen Elizabeth II has died

Queen Elizabeth II, the UK's longest-serving monarch, has died at Balmoral aged 96, after reigning for 70 years.
She died peacefully on Thursday afternoon at her Scottish estate, where she had spent much of the summer.
The Queen came to the throne in 1952 and witnessed enormous social change.

The Line of Succession

Succession to the British throne is determined by descent, gender,
legitimacy, and religion. Under common law, the Crown is inherited by
a sovereign's children or by a childless sovereign's nearest collateral
line.
